Please remember these things




  • You should devote a minimum of 2 hrs in a day for getting into comfort zone..

  • Finger practice is the best way to set your finger according to guitar chords.

  • Practice according to the time mentioned in video.

CAUTION !

  • It maybe frustrating to learn guitar in initial days, even the fingers might hurt. Have patience!
  • Sound quality will come with time and practice, don't expect magic.


    1st Practice

In this video, we introduce Finger Practice.



  • Use the first and secong finger.

  • Use the first and second fret in your guitar.

  • You will need a practice of about 3 hours to gain comfort.

CAUTION !


    2nd Practice



  • Use the first and third finger.

  • Use the first and third fret.

  • You will need a practice of about 3 hours to gain comfort.

    3rd Practice



  • Use the first,second and third fingers.

  • Use the first ,second and third fret

  • You will need a practice of about 3 hours to gain comfort.

    4th Practice



  • Use the first, second, third and fourth finger.

  • Use the first, second, third and fourth fret.

  • You will need a practice of about 3 hours to gain comfort.
